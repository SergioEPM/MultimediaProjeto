#define MAX_DICT_SIZE   65535
 The maximum number of entries allowed in the LZ78 dictionary. Limited to 65535 to fit within an unsigned short (2 bytes).
 
#define HASH_SIZE   131071
 The size of the Hash Table array. Uses a prime number (131071) significantly larger than the dictionary size to minimize hash collisions and maintain O(1) average lookup time.
